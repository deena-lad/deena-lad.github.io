FiLMeR: Resolution and Domain-Invariant Deep Learning Emulation for Regional Weather Forecasting

We introduce FiLMeR, a novel deep learning framework for regional weather emulation that achieves resolution and domain invariance through Feature-wise Linear Modulation (FiLM) conditioning. By leveraging ERA5 reanalysis data and a U-Net backbone with multi-scale skip connections, FiLMeR generalizes across spatial resolutions without retraining — enabling robust short-to-medium range forecasts over heterogeneous geographic domains. Experiments demonstrate competitive skill scores against operational NWP baselines at a fraction of the computational cost.
